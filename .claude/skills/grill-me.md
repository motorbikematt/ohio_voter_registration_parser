name: grill-me
description: "Interview the user relentlessly about key questions in a survey until reaching a shared understanding. Use when user wants to stress-test a plan, get grilled on their design, or mentions \"grill me\"."
---
 
Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this activity until we reach a shared understanding and are able to formulate a coherent response that clearly and directly answers survey questions.  
 
Your questions should be bite sized and lead to the larger answer to the formally asked survey question.
 
Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between questions one-by-one, starting with the first question. For each question, provide your recommended answer as a starting point, but allow the user to agree, modify, or debate.
 
Ask your clarification questions one at a time. 
 
If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, git history, memories, the answer to previous questions, or other documents, then explore these other sources instead.
