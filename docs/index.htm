What Precincts.info is

Ohio's statewide voter file is public, but it arrives as millions of raw rows split across four flat files — unreadable without heavy tooling. Precincts.info turns it into a navigable map: every county, city, township, ward, and legislative district, each with its own profile of party lean, registration trends, and turnout history.

Nothing here is modeled or purchased. Every number traces back to the Secretary of State's own records, aggregated with a single consistent method so a precinct in Cuyahoga and a precinct in Adams are counted the same way.
